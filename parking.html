Main Mela Ground Parking
Mela Road, Near Main Entrance, Gwalior, MP
Located right at the main entrance, this is the most convenient parking for visitors. It is spacious, well-lit, and monitored by security staff 24/7. Easy access to the main fairground and all major attractions.
Race Course Parking
Race Course Road, Gwalior, MP
Ideal for those coming from the city center or railway station. This lot offers ample space, smooth entry/exit, and is a short walk from the mela. Shuttle service available during peak hours.
Padav Parking
Padav Area, Near Railway Station, Gwalior, MP
Perfect for visitors arriving by train. Padav Parking is safe, affordable, and has clear signage to guide you to the mela entrance. Staff assistance is available for luggage and directions.
Phoolbagh Parking
Phoolbagh, Near Bus Stand, Gwalior, MP
Located near the main bus stand, this lot is best for those using public transport. It features covered parking, CCTV surveillance, and a help desk for visitor support.
